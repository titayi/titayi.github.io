How to Prepare Ugali

  1. Boil water
  2. Add some flour
  3. Mix the flour in the boiling water
  4. Add more flour
  5. Continue mixing to get the right texture
  6. Leave it to seamer
  7. Mix it more
  8. When the right texture is achieved, take it off the cooker
  9. Eat and enjoy Ugali
